What's this?

Currently, your computer is executing and evolutionary algorithm that tries to forecast the future values of a time-series.

Is it dangerous?

No.
Everything is being executed in your browser, and we don't store data or programs in your computer.
Furthermore, data being used is related to financial subjetcs, and do not affect to the life or health of any living being.

Who are the authors of this page?

This is a GeNeura Teams's research project.
The web page and most of the JavaScript code have been done by professor Víctor M. Rivas.
The CSS has been taken from template Intensify, by templated.co.

What is this for?

These experiments allows us to evaluate the jsEvRBF library. This library makes radial basis function neural netwroks to evolve.
